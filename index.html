History

This game was invented during a storm. Lost in the middle of nowhere with a pc an old man discover that we can create a game with basic javascript and html that will recreate the hazard of life.
This game aims to show how life is about making decision without always knowing what comes next.

Rules

  • You can choose any spot on the next line
  • Always line by line

  • Choose wisely but listen to your instinct
